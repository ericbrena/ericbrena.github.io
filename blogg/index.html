Why sloth is one of the most incredible animals

Sloths are one of the most amazing creatures on this planet and I am going to tell you exactly why!

First of all it's very important to state that you can never trust a sloth. There are two types of sloth, there is the two-toed sloth and the three-toed sloth. You might be thinking that the two-toed sloth has two toes? WRONG, these devious sloths actually has 3 toes just like the three-toed sloths, but They however only has two fingers! That's crazy!

But not only are the sloths awesome, they also know the definition of chill!

The sloths are a very calm animals, and rightly so because they are awesome. Sloths enjoy spending their time chilling from branches, eating leafs and living the life. But do not approach this awesome creature with ill intent, even tough the sloth is awesome it is a fierce animal. If threatened the sloth will run at you, upside-down, with their massive claws! Now if that isn't awesome I don't know what is!

The sloths are awesome throughout history!

Yes it's true sloths is an amazing animal that no one is even close to compete with, but historically sloths was even more awesome! To begin to fathom the fundamental historical depth of their awesomeness we have to go back to their roots. The sloth family is very old and its forefather; The Megatherium. An ancient sloth that was just as big as an elephant! Roaming the face of earth with claws the size of your forearm. Scary isn't it? But do not worry, we are not living in the past. Even tough the modern sloth might not be as scary as the older one, it is still very much awesome.
